Your data

One last thing: privacy.

That tracking prompt was only a demonstration. Peak will never track your activity across other companies' apps or websites.

You stay in control.

Peak only uses the information you intentionally add to calculate trends and show your records back to you. Profile preferences personalize the app, while friend and league visibility is handled separately.
